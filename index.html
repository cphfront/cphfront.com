Meetup

We meet in Copenhagen each month on tuesdays, wednesdays or thursdays at 19:00 and have talks for 1.5-2 hours with time inbetween and afterwards to chat with fellow developers.
See our events and join our group on
meetup.com/cphfront

Updates

Follow @cphfront for updates.

Hosts

Hosting a meetup is a great oportunity for your company or organization to get in touch with the finest frontend developeres in Copenhagen. You can find more information in the guide for hosts.

Speakers

Talks can be anything between 5-45 minutes on topics somewhat related to frontend development. Speaking at a meetup is a great excuse to learn more about a topic and to practice your presentation skills.
Please don't be shy :)
